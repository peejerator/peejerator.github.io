Power-Aware Systolic MAC Array

Jan — Apr 2026
RTL Designer · FPGA / digital design

Designed a fully synthesizable 3×3 output-stationary systolic MAC array in Verilog with wavefront clock gating and DVFS-style power modes (FULL / HALF / SLEEP) on a Xilinx Zynq UltraScale+. Led the RTL for the activity tracker, clock-gate controller, and mode FSM. Closed timing at 100 MHz (WNS = +6.98 ns) and demonstrated up to 10× lower switching activity versus the ungated baseline, validated across 12 post-implementation power scenarios.
